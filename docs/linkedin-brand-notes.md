# LinkedIn Brand Notes — Option A: The Delivery Architect

## Core positioning
Lead with execution: "I'm the person who makes AI transformation real — not just planned."
Builder-first, confident. For people who respect shipping over strategy.

## Proposed headline
"Director at Deloitte | I make AI transformation real — from architecture to delivery"

## Key differentiators to articulate

1. **Hype vs substance**: Most AI transformation in regulated industries is still marketing.
   Few people have actually shipped working systems at enterprise scale.

2. **Pilots that don't scale**: Lots of POCs. Almost nothing makes it to production.
   The gap is architecture, release management, and adoption planning — not the model.

3. **Change management is the real work**: Organizations treat AI adoption as an installation.
   It's not. The "do-er" gets elevated to reviewer. Roles change. Processes change.
   You have to design the human journey alongside the technical system.

4. **Incremental delivery against maturity**: Implementation should be phased against
   the organization's actual adoption readiness — not the most aggressive roadmap.

## Proposed summary rewrite

I make AI transformation real.

Most organisations have strategy. Few have someone who can architect, plan, and deliver
it — at enterprise scale, within regulatory context, alongside the people who have to
live with it.

With 20+ years across consulting and delivery, I've led the full arc: from roadmap to
architecture to release. I work across industries, but the pattern is always the same —
turn ambition into working systems, and land them in a way that sticks.

Current focus: agentic AI and multi-agent systems for complex, high-stakes environments.
